Profhilo

Skin Rejuvenation
Description:
Profhilo is an injectable treatment that promises benefits like anti-aging, hydration of the skin, care for skin sagging and laxity. It is a purely 100% hyaluronic acid (HA)-based product which utilises a new process of bio-remodelling to target elastin structures in the skin.

Profhilo has been awarded the Expat Living Readers' Choice: Aesthetics Awards 2022 .
How it Works:
0.2ml of Profhilo will be injected into specifically-selected Bio Aesthetic Points (BAPs) on each side of the face. Through this treatment, it can help stimulate collagen, elastin production and increase the elasticity of your skin.
Benefits:
∘ Increases in overall skin’s health
∘ Repairs tissues and scars
∘ Increases skin’s hydration
∘ Smoother and firmer skin
∘ Effective alternative to surgical treatments
Suitable For:
Anyone over the age of 30 who has:
 ∘ Saggy skin
 ∘ Ageing skin
 ∘ Dull looking skin
 ∘ Multiple fine lines and wrinkles
 ∘ Acne scars
How fast can I expect to see the results:
Refreshed appearance may be noticed a week after your first treatment. However, the full results can be seen 4 weeks after your second treatment.

Skin Booster

Skin Booster

Skin Rejuvenation
Description:
Skin Boosters are dermal fillers injected directly into the skin to restore the elasticity of the skin and replenish your skin’s smoothness for a youthful look through small tiny injections of soft hyaluronic acid.



Skin Booster has been crowned winner of the Daily Vanity Awards 2019.
How it Works:
Skin Booster uses hyaluronic acid. When injected into our skin, it causes our skin to absorb water which results in a favourable skin environment and increases hydration.With greater hydration, our skin looks plumper, brighter, and more youthful.
Benefits:
∘ Increases in skin’s hydration
∘ Smoother and brighter skin
∘ Increases skin’s elasticity and firmness
∘ Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
∘ Improves acne scarring
Suitable For:
Young adults who has:
 ∘ Dry looking skin
 ∘ Ageing skin
 ∘ Dull looking skin
 ∘ Acne scars
How fast can I expect to see the results:
Results can be seen from the first treatment. However, Skin Booster needs to be performed once every 3-4 weeks for a total of 3-4 sessions to get the best result. Subsequent treatments are performed every 6 months as maintenance.

Rejuran Healer

Skin Rejuvenation
Description:
Rejuran Healer is the newest aesthetic treatment from Korea and has become one of the world’s most popular treatments. Rejuran Healer (婴儿针), is a skin booster treatment that provides benefits such as anti-aging, skin rejuvenation & regeneration, and hair growth stimulation.

Mole Removal

Skin Rejuvenation
Description:
Moles may be regarded as a beauty mark by some. For others who are insecure of them, we offer clinical solutions to eliminate them safely and effectively through the use of a CO2 Laser.
How it Works:
The CO2 Laser penetrates the top skin layers. It is used to burn off the tissue cells of the mole. The entire procedure takes only 15 minutes, depending on the span of the selected body area.
Benefits:
∘ Shorter downtime as compared to
  deep chemical peeling
∘ Minimises damage to surrounding tissues
Suitable For:
Most healthy adults
How fast can I expect to see the results:
Results are immediate and any superficial scab or crust formed after the procedure falls off after a few days.
