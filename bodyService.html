Cryo Fat Freeze

Body Sculpting
Description:
Cryo Fat Freeze is a non-surgical fat freeze treatment that can help you to remove fats in stubborn areas to achieve your desired figure without the downtime. It reduces the number of fat cells by freezing them, without affecting the overlying skin.

Cryo Fat Freeze has been awarded Best Fat Freezing treatment by Daily Vanity Spa Awards 2021.
How it Works:
Cryo Fat Freeze uses a vacuum and controlled temperature cooling with a smart applicator that has a massage system to promote lymph drainage. After fat cells are cooled, they are released into the lymphatic system and eliminated naturally through the body’s metabolic process.
Benefits:
∘ Reduces fat cells
∘ No downtime
∘ Non-surgical
Suitable For:
Anyone who has:
 ∘ Stubborn fat in problem areas
   (chin, waist, tummy, inner & outer thighs)
How fast can I expect to see the results:
A few weeks is required to see the visible results. Notable effects can be seen over 7-90 days with results lasting 12 months or longer.
