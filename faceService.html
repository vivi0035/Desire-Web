Ellansé

Facial Enhancements
Description:
Being one of the world’s longest lasting fillers, Ellansé (少女针) is a collagen-stimulating filler that eliminates anti-aging signs with comparatively longer lasting effects than other dermal fillers. It eliminates wrinkles and also stimulates your own natural collagen for a more youthful look.

Ellansé has been awarded the Expat Living Readers' Choice: Best Aesthetic Treatment 2022.
How it Works:
Ellansé fillers are made of precisely engineered microspheres from polycaprolactone (PCL). It immediately fills up the volume deficient areas on our faces. Concurrently, the PCL microspheres present in Ellansé stimulates collagen production and thus enforces a long-lasting volumization. Over time, the residual effects of the stimulated collagen will achieve a natural and durable result.
Benefits:
∘ Facial reshaping and contouring
∘ Delays ageing signs
∘ Rejuvenates skin
∘ Lifts skin
∘ Natural stimulated collagen revitalises and firms skin
Suitable For:
Anyone over the age of 20 who has:
 ∘ Ageing skin
 ∘ Multiple fine lines and wrinkles
How fast can I expect to see the results:
Results are immediate and the notable results can last from anywhere between 1-4 years.

HIFU Toning

High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) Toning is a non-invasive ultrasound treatment that offers saggy skin a high performance lifting and tightening effect.

HIFU Toning

Facial Enhancements
Description:
High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) Toning is a non-invasive ultrasound treatment that offers saggy skin a high performance lifting and tightening effect. HIFU is commonly performed for face lifting, fat cheeks removal or double chin Removal to achieve a V Shape Face.

HIFU Toning has been awarded the Expat Living Readers' Choice: Best Anti-Aging Treatment 2021.
How it Works:
HIFU Toning emits ultrasound energy into superficial, deep dermis and SMAS layers of the skin for an effective triple layer lifting effect on the targeted areas.
Benefits:
∘ Zero downtime
∘ Non-invasive
∘ Stimulates collagen production
∘ Tightens skin
Suitable For:
Anyone who has:
 ∘ Saggy skin
 ∘ Ageing skin
 ∘ Dry skin
 ∘ Multiple fine lines and wrinkles
How fast can I expect to see the results:
Notable effects will take around 2-3 months. Collagen production will progressively improve over 4-6 weeks.

Eye Bags Removal

Facial Enhancements
Description:
Eye Bags Removal is an injectable “tear trough filler” procedure that plumps up the undereye area and gives a more rejuvenated look.

Eye Bags Removal uses a micro-insulated needle to deliver heat and radiofrequency to selectively target and shrink subcutaneous fat under the eyes.
Benefits:
∘ Melts excess fats
∘ Tightens skin
∘ Stimulates collagen production
∘ Minimal downtime
Suitable For:
Anyone who has:
 ∘ Excess fats under the eyes
How fast can I expect to see the results:
Some patients do see improvements after 1-2 weeks. However, the best results are approximately after 1 to 2 months.
