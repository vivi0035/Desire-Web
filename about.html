Our Values & Beliefs

Established in 2012, Desire was founded to help people fulfill their desire to better themselves. We believe that everyone has the right to get affordable and safe beauty treatments to be free from insecurities. We want you to have control of your body and appearance in a positive and fulfilling manner.

∘ We believe in providing the highest quality of treatments and experience.
∘ We believe that safety comes first before beauty.
∘ We dedicate ourselves to review the latest research and innovation in the aesthetic medicine field.
∘ We are committed in upholding the authenticity and total transparency of our treatments.

Giving our customers value for their money is important to us. We strive to give you high quality treatments at affordable prices to help you save more in the long run.





Cutting Edge Technology

Desire pride ourselves in our best-of-class equipment and ingredients to provide exceptional results for you using our wide range of services and treatments.

We offer FDA approved CO2 Laser, Ultherapy, and Cryolipolysis for aesthetic treatment in Singapore. Our Ultherapy system’s patented DeepSEE® transducers provide simultaneous visualisation and treatment which enables precision targeting, patient customisation, and enhanced comfort.

We go above and beyond to procure clinical grade ingredients from reputable suppliers across the globe. Our facials contain active extract, and our signature Ellansé filler uses precisely engineered polycaprolactone (PCL) from the Netherlands. The hyaluronic acid (HA) used in our Chin Fillers and Profhilo treatment is dermatologist-approved.
